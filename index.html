Introduction to Astronautics

Astronautics is the science and technology of spaceflight and the exploration of space. It encompasses the design, construction, and operation of spacecraft, as well as the mission planning and data analysis. Simple Astronautics aims to provide an easy-to-understand introduction to this exciting field without the complex mathematics.

Sejarah penerbagan luar angkasa

The history of spaceflight is a tale of human curiosity and ingenuity, from the early rocketry experiments to the exploration of our solar system. We will explore the key milestones and achievements in the history of spaceflight.

Desain roket

Rocket design involves the engineering of vehicles capable of traveling through space. We will discuss the principles of rocketry, including propulsion, stability, and guidance, with a focus on the Angara rocket family.

Mekanik orbit

Orbital mechanics is the study of the motion of objects in space. We will cover the basics of orbital mechanics, including types of orbits, transfer maneuvers, and interplanetary travel.

Orang-Orang penting di astronautika

Many individuals have contributed to the advancement of astronautics. We will highlight some of the key figures and their significant roles in the field, with short biographies.
